About Twin Heart Event

Your Happiness - Our Dream.!!!

Welcome to Twin Heart Event Planner, based in Thoothukudi! We provide comprehensive event planning services across Tamil Nadu at affordable prices. From invitations to the final farewell ("பத்திரிக்கை முதல் பந்தி வரை"), we handle everything to make your special day perfect.

With several years of experience under our belts, we have the skills and expertise to handle every aspect of event planning from venue selection to catering including lots of fun and entertainment. Our experienced team of professionals is dedicated to bringing your vision to life, whether it's a small, intimate gathering or a grand gala.

We have successfully registered our firm under MSME. We have started our journey on May 26th, 2021 and founded by Ms. Abinaya A, MCA. Our team is spread across various locations to serve your events wherever they may be.
